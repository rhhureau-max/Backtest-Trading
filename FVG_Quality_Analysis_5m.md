# FVG Quality Analysis - 5-Minute Data (2018-2025)

## Overview

This document presents the Fair Value Gap (FVG) quality analysis for 5-minute trading data at the 8:30 AM candle from 2018 to 2025.

### Analysis Methodology

- **FVG Detection**: Based on three candles (8:25, 8:30, 8:35)
- **Quality Assessment**: Checks if price returns to FVG zone within 5 candles after FVG closes (candles 8:40-9:05)
- **Good FVG**: Price does NOT return to the FVG zone within 5 candles
- **Bad FVG**: Price DOES return to the FVG zone within 5 candles

## Key Insights

1. **Overall Pattern**: Approximately 60% of FVG gaps get filled within 5 candles at the 5-minute timeframe.

2. **Best Years**: 2018-2019 (48-49% good FVG) - most favorable period for FVG holds.

3. **Worst Year**: 2024 (34.29% good FVG) - most challenging year.

4. **Market Regime Change**: Clear shift after 2020, with good FVG rates declining from ~49% to ~35-37%.

5. **Recovery Signal**: 2025 showing strong recovery to 45.87%, approaching pre-2020 levels.

6. **Stability Metrics**: 2022-2023 showed identical statistics (37.40%), indicating market stability period.
