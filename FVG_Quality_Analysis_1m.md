# FVG Quality Analysis - 1-Minute Data (2018-2025)

## Overview

This document presents the Fair Value Gap (FVG) quality analysis for 1-minute trading data at the 8:30 AM candle from 2018 to 2025.

### Analysis Methodology

- **FVG Detection**: Based on three candles (8:29, 8:30, 8:31)
- **Quality Assessment**: Checks if price returns to FVG zone within 5 candles after FVG closes (candles 8:32-8:36)
- **Good FVG**: Price does NOT return to the FVG zone within 5 candles
- **Bad FVG**: Price DOES return to the FVG zone within 5 candles

5. **Recent Trend**: 2024-2025 shows a recovery pattern after 2023's decline.

6. **Bullish vs Bearish**: Bullish FVG slightly more prevalent but both show similar fill rates.

## Trading Implications

- **Gap Fill Expectation**: Plan for ~60% probability of gap fill within 5 candles
- **Hold Probability**: ~40% of FVG gaps provide reliable support/resistance
- **Year-Specific Factors**: Market conditions significantly impact FVG quality
- **Risk Management**: Essential given the 60/40 distribution
