## Recommendations for Traders

### Optimal Approach

1. **Primary Analysis**: Focus on 15-minute for best reliability
2. **Confirmation**: Use 5-minute for additional confirmation
3. **Entry Timing**: Can use 1-minute for precise entries
4. **Year Awareness**: Adjust expectations based on current year performance

### When to Avoid FVG Trading

- During years with < 35% good FVG rate
- On 1-minute timeframe during volatile markets

- During major macro events (similar to COVID impact)

### When FVG Trading is Most Profitable

- 15-minute timeframe in stable markets
- Years following major corrections (e.g., 2025 after 2024)
- When multiple timeframes align
- During recovery phases from volatility
