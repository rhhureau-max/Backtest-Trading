# FVG Maximum Price Excursion Analysis - Multi-Window (1M)

## Overview

Analysis of maximum price excursion for Good FVG on **1m** timeframe across multiple candle windows.

**Window Sizes Analyzed:** 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 50 candles

**Methodology:**
- Identifies FVG at 8:30 AM candle
- Filters for Good FVG (price doesn't return to FVG zone within window)
- Measures maximum price movement from close of 3rd candle
- Analyzes across different window sizes
- Nasdaq: 1 tick = 0.25 points
